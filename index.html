About Me

Born and raised in South Orange, NJ. I wrestled for four years in high school and continued in college where i picked up Brazillian Jiu Jitsu, Judo, and Muay Thai. I graduated from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University where i studied aviation and received a degree in Aviation Business Administration. Since then i have been working in the finance industry and am currently employed by Morgan Stanley.

When im not working or watching Netflix, i enjoy watching hulu. When im not doing any of that im usually working out, out with friends somewhere interesting (or not), or eating.
